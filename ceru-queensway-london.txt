# RESTAURANT INFO:
Embark on a culinary journey through the sunny and fertile region of the Eastern Mediterranean at CERU.
Located just a stone’s throw away from the Central Line tube station of Queensway and the District line underground station of Bayswater. North side of Hyde park, next to Nothing Hill.

We open daily for lunch & dinner serving seasonal menu alongside daily brunch until 5pm, cocktails, as well handpicked wines and fresh homemade lemonades. Our menu of big avors and small plates distills all the zest and spice across the Eastern Mediterranean, The restaurant seats 47, including 6 bar seats.

Queensway will soon be home to The Whiteley London - the forthcoming redevelopment of this iconic London landmark including UK’s rst Six Senses Hotel. With Hyde Park on our door step and major attractions nearby, we are the ideal destination for diners on their way to the Royal Albert Hall, as well as a welcome oasis to the weary shopper at the end of a day spent in the boutiques of Notting Hill or Kensington.

The Fusion Frontier: A NEW WORLD OF FLAVOURS & TECHNIQUES
We combine the traditional flavours and techniques of the region with innovative and modern culinary practices.
Enjoy the best of both worlds - an adventurous exploration of new taste combinations while still remaining true to the heart and soul of the area.
The menu showcases a diverse range of dishes, inspired by the travels of founders Barry and Patricia Hilton throughout the Eastern Mediterranean. Bursting with vibrant flavours and ingredients, it has been a source of enjoyment and inspiration for many years.
